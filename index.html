Choco Candy

By Brinda

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Chocolate
  • Baking soda

Instructions

  1. Step 1: Start by caramalizing the sugar.
  2. Step 2: Then add the chocolate and baking soda into the caramalized sugar.
  3. Step 3: Once turned golden brown pour in into a mould and let it cool.
